**PRIVACY POLICY**  
**PFT & Spirometry Tool**  
**Last updated:** [Month Day, Year]

Abhishek Kumar (“Developer,” “we,” “us”) operates the mobile application **PFT & Spirometry Tool** (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

**1. Summary**

The App is designed for **trained healthcare professionals** as an **educational PFT pattern reference**. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit patient health information (PHI) on our servers. **We do not operate backend servers** for App functionality. Interpretation and report history run **locally on your device**.

**2. Information we do not collect**

We do **not** ask for or collect from you directly:

- Patient names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, or other PHI  
- Email addresses (except as handled by Apple when you purchase a subscription)  
- Location data  
- Contacts, photos, or microphone/camera data  
- Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking  

**Do not enter patient identifiers** into the App. You are responsible for compliance with HIPAA, PIPEDA, GDPR, and applicable institutional policies.

**3. Information stored locally on your device**

The App may store on your device only:

- PFT values and notes **you enter** (under your control)  
- Generated interpretation text and saved report history  
- Optional de-identified labels for reports  
- App preferences (e.g. terms acceptance version, subscription state via Apple)  
- **Audit trail** of anonymous usage events (app launch, interpretation performed, report exported)—**no PHI**, used for compliance logging only; exportable from the Disclaimers tab  

You may delete the App to remove locally stored data. We cannot remotely delete data on your device.

**4. Subscriptions and purchases**

Purchases are processed by **Apple** through your Apple ID. We receive only what Apple provides to developers (e.g. subscription status, product identifiers, purchase dates)—not your full payment card details. Apple’s privacy practices are described at:  
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**5. Email and sharing**

If you email a PDF report, sending occurs through **your device’s Mail app** and **your email account**. We do not receive the contents of emails you send.

**6. Third-party services**

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or crash reporting SDKs at the time of this policy. If this changes, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy label.

**7. Children**

The App is not intended for use by patients or the general public, and not directed to children under 13 (or 16 where applicable).

**8. Data retention and security**

Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. We recommend device passcode/biometric lock and institutional policies for devices used in clinical environments.

**9. International users**

The App is offered from Canada. If you use the App outside Canada, you consent to local-device processing as described here and are responsible for lawful use in your jurisdiction.

**10. Changes**

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
